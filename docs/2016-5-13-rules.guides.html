(private, static) findOrCreateAxis(scale, defs) → {void}

Attempts to find an axis for the given scale. If one is not found, and the enclosing group does not already have two axes of the same type (i.e., two x-axes or two y-axes), a new Lyra axis Guide primitive is constructed.
