Primitive

A Lyra Primitive.

Constructor

new Primitive()

Lyra primitives are wrappers around corresponding Vega definitions. Lyra-specific properties are denoted with an underscore prefix and are removed during clean exports. Each Lyra primitive is given a unique identifier, and these identifiers are used to store references to other primitives.
